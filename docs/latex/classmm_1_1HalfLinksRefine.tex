\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Refine a region of nodes {\ttfamily region} by connecting every node in {\ttfamily region} to its support domain and generating new nodes at half distances. 

The new nodes are filtered to meet a minimum distance criterion to prevent points that would be too close.
